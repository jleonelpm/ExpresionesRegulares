
The Northern Exposure Music Guide                         (version 2004.07.07)

This list was reconstructed in July 2004 from a variety of internet sources,
including the World Wide Web Archive of Debi Richards' original list,
as well as USENET posts.  It is not complete by any means.  As I am not
responsible for any of the entries below, I would like to give credit
where credit is due, so if you were an original contributor to this guide
and are not listed below, please contact Kipp Teague via the feedback form
on http://www.retroweb.com .  Also, if you have additions and corrections
for the guide, please send them using the same form.

Original contributors to this guide:
	Debi Richards (now Deb Thomson) (primary)
	Art Pattison (season 6 and more additions)
